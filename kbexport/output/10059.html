The GRITS batch report causes EncounterPRO client to hang

10059

Introduction

Symptom

When you run the GRITS batch report, EncounterPRO will freeze or hang.

Cause

This is caused by the sheer volume of data that is being collected by the GRITS immunzation report.

Workaround

Try running batch reports for smaller increments of time.  One month usually works well.
