Billing Accepted icon missing

10086

Introduction

this is to explain the different icons used for showing billing status in check out screen.

Symptom

checkout screen patient shows out with green tick marks and not turning to a $ bill icon.

Cause

the most common reason for this is , encounterpro not receing acknowledgments back from billing system about the status for the charge message. only few billing systems send the status of the bill [whether it's accepted or rejected] and may be the PMS not sending acks and so the icons are not changing from green tick mark to $ bill.

Workaround

Resolution

demand PMS to send acks for the Encounterpro Bills.

both developers from encounterpro and PMS agreed to do this enhancement.
