Adding rooms

10088

Introduction

This article explains how to add rooms to EncounterPRO.

At this time, there is no way to do this thru the interface. This mustbe done thru the database, either in SQL Enterprise Manager, or in MSAccess cpr.mdb.

Symptom


Cause

This will usually involve only inserting rows into two tables: o_group_rooms and o_rooms.

O_rooms holds the info specifying things like: room_id, what the nameof the room is, what its type is, what order the room shows in relationto other rooms.

O_group_rooms specifies what rooms show in which groups by matching a room_id with a group_id.

Groups are the tabs on the right side of the Office view. Examples of standard groups are Office, Telephone, Chart Room.
O_Groups specifies group names and group_ids. This table is usuallyuseful only for knowing what group_id goes with which group name.

When a request comes in to add a room that requires adding a group, a row will have to be inserted into o_groups.

Sometimes a request will be specific to a particular location of apractice that has multiple locations. In this case, table c_office willalso have to be taken into account.

Workaround


Resolution

Example 1:
Add an exam room to the office tab. This will be the 3rd exam room in the office.
If the group_id of the office tab is 109 (after finding this out from o_groups), go to o_rooms, insert a row where
    room_id=EXAM3,
    room_name=Exam 3,
    room_sequence is greater than the first two exam rooms,
    room_type= that of other exam rooms (usually $EXAMINATION)
    room_status=OK
    office_id= that of other exam rooms (usually 0001)
    status=OK
Next, go to o_group_rooms and insert a row where group_id=109 and room_id=EXAM3

Example 2:
There are two Waiting rooms already added. The practice wants to get rid of those and have just one Waiting room.
In O_rooms set the status (not the room_status) of the already existingWaiting rooms to NA. Add a row to o_rooms, like the already existingWaiting rooms where room_type=$WAITING.
NOTE: Waiting rooms do not require a row in o_group_rooms or o_groups.
