Completing, Deleting, Cancelling To do Items or Msgs in VS 2.0 and VS 4.0

10108

Introduction

 

 This article provides information on Completing, Deleting, Cancelling To do Items or Msgs in VS 2.0 and VS 4.0

Symptom

Todo Items can't be completed

Treatment will not close on Outstanding Labs and Test screen

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

Completing a to do item for Version 4 Epro

  1. Get “User_id” from “c_user” (User requesting item to be completed)
  2. Open p_patient_wp_item
  3.  Filter for “Ordered_by” = “User_ID”
  4.  Exclude Status of “COMPLETED”, “CANCELLED”, and “Expired”
  5.  Locate correct to do item by searching for correct criteria Description and\or ordered_service or cpr_id.
  6. Change Status from “Dispatched” or “Started” to “COMPLETED” on to do item\s that need to be completed. 
  7. Enter Today’s date in “End_Date” field
  8. Enter in User_Id in “Completed_By
  9. Refresh To\do item screen and ensure that to do item is completed

Cancelling a to do item for Version 4 Epro (In Build 33.7 and higher you can click on icon on the to do and cancel the to do item)

 

  1. Follow steps 1-5
  2. Change Status from “Dispatched” or “Started” to “CANCELLED” on to do item\s that need to be cancelled. 
  3. Follow Steps 7-9

 

Completing a to do item for Version 2.0 Epro

  1. Get “User_id” from “c_user” (User requesting item to be completed)
  2. Open u_todo_list
  3. Filter for “c_user” = “User_ID”
  4. Exclude Status of “COMPLETED”, “CANCELLED”
  5. Locate correct to do item by searching for correct criteria Description and\or ordered_service or cpr_id
  6. Change Status from “READY” or “NOTREADY” to “COMPLETED” on to do item\s that need to be completed. 
  7. Enter Today’s date in “Start_date_time
  8. Refresh To\do item screen and ensure that to do item is completed
  9. If the to do item that needs to be closed is a Lab, Test or XRAY, you must locate that treatment in p_treatment and set the Treatment_status to "CLOSED", "End_date" = todays date, Close_encounter" = Last Direct Encounter visit.

Removing a To do item in VS 2.0

 

  1. Follow steps 1-5
  2. Delete entire record. We must have email or fax confiirmation to do this step
  3. Ensure to do item is deleted
