Refering provider not sent to billing system from EPRO

10111

Introduction

This article explains about transferring referring providers to PM systems.

Symptom

a) Referring providers are not received with charges

b) Elligence system receiving complaints that it's showing the attending provider as the referring provider.

Cause

EncounterPRO never sends referring providers with charges

Workaround

Tell the PM systems to look up for referring providers from their database because EncounterPRO will not send them

Resolution

EncounterPRO will not send referring providers with charges. If they need to get that info then PM system is the best sourceto get the referring provider for the patients. Of course it's alsostored in EncounterPRO but it's used only for informational purpose andwe will send that out with charges.
