Lab test not billing

10112

Introduction

This is to explain why some of the lab/test observations are not billing

Symptom

Selecting a lab/test observation will not generate a billable collect or perform procedure code in billing screen

Cause

For that lab/test observation they might not have attached the collect and perform procedures.

Workaround


Resolution

a) Version 2 Sites:

Go to configuration->exam,labs&tests

Select the apporpriate observation type and category and look forthe specific (non-billable) observation. Go to edit screen and seewhether a collect or perform procedure is attached. If that is ain-house procedure just attach a collection procedure [ask them whichone to attach it] or assign both collect and perform procedure codes.

 

b) Version 4 Sites

Go into observations and repeat the same. Get help from configuration specialist if you are not familiar.
