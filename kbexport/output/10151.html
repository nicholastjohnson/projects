Patient Material locking Epro for word documents

10151

Introduction

Symptom

When a patient education material is opened in word and the user tried to exit out of the application, EncounterPRO locks up. and It take 5-10 min to go back to normal

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

1. Go to treatment types and search for patient materials

2. Select patient materials and edit treatment type

3. Find out the default workplan for this treatment type

4. Go to worklans, change the workplan type to treatments

5. Search for the default workplan for patient materials (ex: patient material for clinical staff)

6. Edit the workplan (you may have to create a local copy)

7. Select configure step, keep selecting next until you get to the "synchronous" and "offer the i'll be back option" choices

8. For both, set the answer to no. (The other screens can be left blank)
