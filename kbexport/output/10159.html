Resolution

We can open the following formats:

JPEG, PNG, TIFF, PCX, TGA, BMP, PSD, PCD, GIF, WMF, EMF, FlashPix

The following TIFF compression formats are supported:

CCIT G4, CCIT G3, CCIT Modified Huffman, Deflate, JPEG, Macintosh Packbits, and LZW
