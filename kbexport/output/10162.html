Changing user color to something specific

10162

Introduction

Can't find just the right shade of fuscia for that finicky doctor's service bars?

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

Find the color of choice at

http://www.december.com/html/spec/colorcodes.html

and plugged the hex value (without the #) into

http://www.computertorture.com/noncompliant/base_calculator.html#

and click on the Convert from Hexidecimal link.

Then paste the decimal value into c_user.color
