Patients checked in twice thru integration

10165

Introduction

This article explains the possible reasons why the patient shows uptwice in waiting room.

Symptom

Patients checked in twice thru an integration interface

Cause

 May be one in waiting room and one in nowhereroom

 Mostly this is caused by the (EPROSERVER) background servicenot terminated properly and running 2 times.

Workaround


 

Resolution

To fix this, stop theservice and make sure all the eproserver.exe instances are gone fromtask manager and then restart it. If it happens frequently, let Sumathi or Chris know.

Also run this query to identify how many patients duplicated thru check in interface and their encounter dates.

selectcount(*),cpr_id,encounter_type,created_by,convert(varchar(10),encounter_date,101)from p_patient_encounter group bycpr_id,encounter_type,created_by,convert(varchar(10),encounter_date,101)having count(*) > 1 and created_by='#SCHED' order byconvert(varchar(10),encounter_date,101)
