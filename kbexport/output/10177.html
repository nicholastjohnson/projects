Centricity Millbrook upgrade problems

10177

Introduction

This article explains what to do about problems caused by a practice upgrading from CPO OPM 03 to CPO OPM 04 SP1 Centricity.

Symptom

All patients check in under the same Dr or under a wrong provider.

 

In Centricity, in Scheduling, doubleclicking on a pt's appointment shows the patient has a Resource assigned and a Provider assigned AND the two are not the same.

Cause

Centricity sends the Provider info but not the Resource info.

Workaround

For each patient checked in, practice can delete the Provider info.

Resolution

Centricity will fix this bug at some point in time.
