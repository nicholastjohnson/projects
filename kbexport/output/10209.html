Finding a Missing Patient

Go to p_patient and check the Patient_status. Youwill usually find that the patient status is set to something otherthen active. You can also advise the patient that they search bydifferent Patient_status on the CHARTS screen.

Finding a Missing Encounter

This usually happens because a user checked thepatient out or cancelled the encounter. In 4.05.34 we can limit theseicons to superusers. The below steps give advise on how to look in thetables to discover who sent the patient to Checkout (EXIT Button) orwho selected on the Cancelled Icon.

  1. Open up p_patient_encounter and search for correct account #.
  2. Write down Encounter_id
  3. Open up p_patient_encounter_progress and search for encounter_id
  4. There will a record with a Progress_type of CLOSEDif the patient was just sent to CHECKOUT. The User_ID is who sent thepatient to CHECKOUT.
