Changing or adding a new treatment on a well D X

10219

Introduction

This article explains how the visit codes are generated.

Symptom

closing a treatment or ordering a refill on a well diagnosis during sick encounter bills a well code instead of sick.

Cause

if an encounter is having WELL & SICK diagnosis then it'll treat the visit as WELL visit.

Workaround

Yes. this is the behaviour of the system. if an encounter is mixedwith SICK AND WELL billable diagnosis then it treats them as WELLvisit.

 

"To change the default behaviour and bill the visit code based onencounter type then go to c_encounter_type and fill in "SICK' incoding_mode for that particular encounter_type. after that, no matterwhat diagnosis ordered it always try to bill them as SICK visit". thisfeature is available with 33.15 and higher versions.
