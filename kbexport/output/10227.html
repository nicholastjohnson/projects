Close a patient outstanding Lab or Test from Utilities in 2.0

10227

Introduction

Symptom

Need to clear a outstanding lab under utilities. This usually needs to be done when a practice cannot close a to do item or a lab themselves.

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

  1. Get the dates and times of the labs. Also the patient name, and their billing #.
  2. Open up u_todo_List and search for lab with specific date by filtering with patients cpr_id
  3. dbo_u_Todo_List
    user_idservicetodo_itemstatusordered_byfoldercpr_id
    JMJ1JIM_COPENHAVERLABRESULTSLab Results For Anne RogersCOMPLETEJMJ1JIM_COPENHAVER
    		3377
  4. Change status to COMPLETE
  5. Locate lab in  "P_treatment_item" by filtering for date, cpr_id, and description
  6. dbo_p_Treatment_Item
    cpr_idtreatment_typetreatment_descriptiontreatment_statuscreatedcreated_by
    10987TESTChem 19CLOSED
    		3/25/1998 7:38:10 PMJMJ1JIM_COPENHAVER
  7. Set treatment_status to CLOSED
