Change a Triage room to an Examination room type

10235

Introduction

Symptom

The Change Rooms menu keeps appearing after Checkin or after Vitals when the patient is in the Nursing Station or Triage Room.

Cause

The Encounter Workplan's steps are set to occur in $EXAMINATION rooms and the room is set as $TRIAGE

Workaround

Resolution

In o_rooms, change the room type from $TRIAGE to $EXAMINATION.
