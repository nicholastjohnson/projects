How to Map a Network Drive

10241

Introduction

This article explains how to map a network drive.This may be analternative way for connecting to a site that is otherwise inaccessible.

Symptom


Cause


Workaround


Resolution

  1. Find the IP address of the computer you wish to map a networkdrive to. Next, from your computer, right click on the My Computer iconand select Map Network Drive.
  2. Make a note of the Drive letter automatically selected. This isthe drive you will go to later to access this remote computer's drive.
  3. In the Folder box, enter the IP address ("IP.address") and drive letter ("L") on the remote computer you wish to connect to in this format: \\IP.address\L$
  4. Click Finish. If this succeeds, you'll have a new drive letter under My Computer.
