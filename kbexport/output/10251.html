Selecting default specialty for Refer Patient screen

10251

Introduction

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

When you click on "Patient Referred To:" or "Specimen Sent To:" and get the Refer Patient for Treatment screen, the specialty that this screen defaults to is determined by treatment type in c_treatment_type.referral_specialty_id.

 

Example:  Whenever you order an x-ray and determine where the patient should be sent to, and you click on the "Patient Referred to" property, the specialty that the screen defaults to is "Radiology."  Well, there aren't any radiologists in your area, but you do have two local hospitals that will take Xrays for physicians, so you want to change the default to Hospitals.  First, look in c_specialty to see that the id for Hospitals is $HOSP3.  Then go to c_treatment_type and find the record for XRAY and set the field referral_specialty_id to $HOSP3.
