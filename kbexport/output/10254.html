Cancelled Treatment is being sent to JMJ Report Service and is getting hung

10254

Introduction

  

Symptom

Report service is hung and the job that is stuck is a Treatment that has been cancelled

Cause

Sometimes when a treatment that is ordered through a workplan is cancelled; it sends the job to the report service.  

Workaround

You can turn off the prompt for reason by either supplying the reason as an attribute or by adding an attribute to suppress the prompt:

Add the attribute to the service in the workplan (c_Workplan_Item_Attribute):

To supply the reason:

Attribute:           progress

Value:               Whatever you want the cancellation reason to be (e.g. “Cancelled by workplan”)

To suppress the prompt:

Attribute:           prompt_for_progress

Value:               False

 

Resolution

This is resolved with Build 34 and EPRO Server 2.5
