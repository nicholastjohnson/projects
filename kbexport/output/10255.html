Clicking on the left side of Left records results in Bilateral

10255

Introduction

Symptom

When you click on the left side of an observation location (like Left or Right), the Bilateral location displays the result.

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

The solution is to go into each child observation and set the common results with no result selected.

This does mean that when you click on the left of "Eyes" at the top level, those child observations can not be defaults.

In the case below, I clicked on the left side of EARS - all of the EAR default results that appear below with a result of "Bilateral" will experience the behavior that Renee described.

In these cases, we need to speak with the doctors to explain this behavior and try to figure out the way that best saves them clicks - they can either have the default set at the top level, or not have a default set at the child level.
