Duplicate tickets created in Centricity for PA's encounters

10256

Introduction

This article explains why the duplicate tickets are generated in Centricity while posting charges.

Symptom


Cause

Duplicate tickets are generated because,

a) charges are going out to bill under PA's account

b) the patient is MANUALLY checked in. While posting the charges,Centricity matches with existing visit record by DOS, & scheduleddoctor. If the scheduled doctor is different from the encounter ownerit'll then duplicate the tickets. This is the behavior of theCentricity system.

Workaround

Version 2

Make sure they are version 2.21.44 and the interface PBDs are up to date. Look up the latest PBDs in FTP site in this folder:
EP_Integrations\Version 2.21\2.21.44
Copy the files to the EncounterPRO installed folder.  (Make sureyou rename the old pbd before copying the new one to the folder.)

If the encounter owner is not assigned wtih "billing id" then assigna supervisor to bill all those provider encounters under the supervisoraccount.

Version 4

Versions higher than 33.7 is not a problem. For 33.7 there is a fixin FTP site. For 33.7 and below, this can happen if the charges aregoing out under PA's name instead their supervisors..

Resolution

a) Upgrade them EproServer 2.2 or higher

b) If they are on version 33.7 then try installing the fixes from 33.7 folder in FTP site.
