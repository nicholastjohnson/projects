Setting decimal numbers to display a leading zero

10273

Introduction

Symptom

Results and dosages less than 1 print without a leading zero (ex. .3 instead of 0.3)

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

in c_unit, in the column display_mask, for all the values that look like #.# or #.## etc, replace the first # with 0 (0.#, 0.##)
