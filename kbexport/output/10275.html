Making a Word report directly available in EncounterPRO

10275

Introduction

This article explains how to take a MS Word report that is available in Epro and making it part of the reports menu so that it shows in epro, not as a Word report.

Symptom


Cause


Workaround


Resolution

  1. Go to c_report_definition, add a row for this report, set component_id=WORD_REPORT
