Epro starts in full screen

10318

Introduction

 

Symptom

Epro starts in full screen on a computer even after changing the preference (window_start_maximized) to N

Cause

 

Workaround


Resolution

Look up the computer_id in o_computers.

Filter for that computer_id number in the preference_key field in o_preferences.

For preference_id "window_state", look at the preference_value andmake sure it does not say maximized at the end of the 4 coordinates.

 

 

Also, make sure there is not a record that has preference_id "window_start_maximized" and value = Y
