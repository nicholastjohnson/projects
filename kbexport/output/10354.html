Introduction

How to Combine several observations on a flowsheet

Symptom

Typically, when users have installed the lab results integration, they also desire the lab results to show on the flowsheets. The RTF command Patient Observations used for the Flowsheets, obtains the composite header observation. By reviewing this header observation in Configuration/Observations, you will see the list of detail observations to be collected under the composite observation on the flowsheet. Click on a detail observation, then click on the equivalence button. Search for the lab result observation from the lab integration. These observations will not be assigned to a speciality or a category. Add these observations as equivalent observations. Then check the flowsheet, to verify that that observation is what you wanted.
