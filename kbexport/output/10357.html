Introduction

 

Symptom

Problem:  a workplan is supposed to order

  • Physical 1 for children in the "1 day - 12 months" age category
  • Physical 2 for children in the "1 year - 2 years" age category,
  • Physical 3 for children in the "3 years - 6 years" age category, and 
  • Physical 4 for children in the "7 years +" age category. 

However, if a child is between his second and third birthdays, or between her sixth and seventh birthdays, a physical will not be ordered.
