Adding a parameter to a display command

10380

Introduction

Symptom

Example: You need to display just the encounter notes that have the progress type of Reviewed and the Progress Key of Physician Review. 

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

Create a display command Encounter Progress.  When the screen comes up where you can enter the progress type, click on the "edit" button at the bottom of the screen under the GUID.  Click on "Add New Param" at the bottom - Param Class is Note Type, Param Title is Progress key, Attribute 1 is progress_key, and Rqd is N.  Fix the numbers in the sort order column so that the paramaters/attributes appear in the correct order.
