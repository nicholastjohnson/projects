Create New Encounter with Alert

10390

Introduction

"We are putting alert notes in EPRO but want the alert to pop up when we are selecting "New Encounter".  Is there a way to make this happen?"

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

Create a new Patient Out of Office workplan, called Check-In with Alert

Add step - Alert - Edit step- Add Service - Alerts - ordered for Workplan Owner, flags = OYNYNNNNNN

2nd step - Check-In - Add Service - Check In Patient - ordered for Workplan Owner, flags = OYNYNNNNNN


Edit the menu at the bottom of the demographic screen, edit the current New Encounter menu item, copy the contents of the Button field to paste later, click on the Menu Item: Check In Patient, click Patient, scroll down and click on Order Workplan, click on the Select Workplan box, click the In Office button, search for the workplan that you just made, click next, highlight "button_workflow.bmp" and paste in the old Check in button, and click Finished.
