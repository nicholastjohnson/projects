Referring Provider send to No Specialty

10404

Introduction

This article describes how to troubleshoot and fix an issue where thereferring provider from a PM system is always sent to No Specialty.

Symptom

In Configuration, Specialties & Consultants, the referring provider from a PM system is always sent to No Specialty.

Cause

1- The specialty is not in c_specialty
2- The PM system is not sending the specialty info

Workaround

Get a copy of the pt demographics file to see what is the cause.
