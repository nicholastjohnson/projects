Cause

Workaround

Resolution

Look in our FTP site under EP_Integrity_Check_Scripts to see the various integrity check scripts.  This includes checking the patient integrity in EncounterPro database, integrity check between EncounterPro vs Centricity etc.

Based on the results of integrity checks, if you find a list of duplicate patients then send the list to the practice and ask them to merge those patients. Build 34 includes a feature for "practice administrator" to execute and merge accounts. Be cautious in merging accounts. Here is the syntax for merging patients.

sp_patient_merge "",""

Example: sp_patient_merge "100","200" - This links all the data associated with cpr_id '200' to cpr_id '100' and sets the cpr_id '200' to "merged" status.
