Adding a new drug vaccine maker

10415

Introduction

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

To add a new vaccine maker, first go to c_drug_maker and add a short maker_id and then the description in maker_name.  Click on another row and the id field will auto-fill. 

The maker can now be added to the vaccine through the program - Configuration | Vaccines and Diseases - click on the vaccine and then the Maker button.
