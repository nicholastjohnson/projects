Displaying dispositions after 'I'll be back'

10423

Introduction

This script will display all attribute records for ToDos and messages done during an encounter.  It doesn't look good, but it will help until the ToDo coding is fixed (I think in 4.05.35) to display everything entered in the disposition after the first 'I'll be back'

Workaround

select
u.user_short_name as By_user,
a.attribute ,
COALESCE (a.value, a.message) as Message,
a.created as Time
from
p_patient_wp_item_attribute a
join p_patient_wp_item  p on
p.patient_workplan_item_id = a.patient_workplan_item_id
join c_user u on
a.created_by = u.user_id
where
a.cpr_id = %cpr_id%
and a.attribute in ('disposition', 'message', 'message_subject')
and p.encounter_id = %encounter_id%
and p.ordered_service in ('TODO', 'SENDTODO', 'MESSAGE')

order by a.patient_workplan_item_id asc, a.created asc
