Adding an image to an observation

10424

Introduction

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

First, the image needs to be in Patient Materials in an editable format (bmp, jpeg preferable)

Configure the observation - at the level you want the image attached, click on the "Image" button in the lower right quadrant of the screen.  Choose the image.

The image box will display in all instances of the observation, and each observation will retain changes made to it in that observation.

Removing the image from the observation template requires table work.
