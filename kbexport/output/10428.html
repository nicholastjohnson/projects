Introduction

This article explains the different services installed and setup in Shands

Symptom

a) Demographics / check in not working

b) Life Time Clinical records (LCR) not working

c) Charges not working

Cause

Once in a while the socket interface goes idle and not import or export messages.

Workaround

It's a TCP/IP based communication. Sometimes the ports goes idle and requires a restart of the following services. [if the frequency is high then it needs to be looked by Development]

a) EncounterPRO Service

b) EproSocket demo-checkin (imports all the demopgraphics and check in)

c) EproSocket Send-Lcr ( exports the clinical records ie reports)

Resolution

LCR is Down:

a) port#30443 is down

if they call and notify that they are getting pages saying that the port is down then usually that means either we are not exporting messages or the get sometimes a notification if there are no activity on the port# for a certain period of time. the best way to look whether everything is working is by looking in o_message_log table to see the records for message_type = "REPORTHL7_LCR". if you notice any messages with a status of "LCR_ACK" then that means it's a problem either on the port or our end. usually this is taken care if you restart the "EproSocketSendLCR" service. if you do not see anything with "LCR_ACK" or "STREAMED" for the above message type then it's a false alarm to them due to inactivity.
