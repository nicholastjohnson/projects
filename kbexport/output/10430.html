Patient does not appear in search results

10430

Introduction

 

Symptom

When the user puts criteria in the patient search screen that results in many records displayed, sometimes the desired patient does not appear in the list, even though their name should fall alphabetically within the names that are displayed.

Cause

This is caused by the search procedure on the sever limiting the patient search results to 100 names.  The results of the query are not always returned in alphabetical order, so a patient which should appear in the first 100 name alphabetically may still get left out.

Workaround

Apply Hotfix 91

Resolution

This issue is resolved in mod level 127
