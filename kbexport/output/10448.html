Check duplicate billing id's

10448

Introduction

This article explains how to check for duplicate billing id's in EncounterPRO patient database

Symptom

a) Patient not checking in

b) Patient updates not working

Cause

If you notice of any of the above symptoms, the patient records are either duplicated or inactivated in EPRO.

Workaround

Resolution

Run the following query to see whether the billing id's are duplicated:

select count(*),billing_id from p_patient group by billing_id having count(*) > 1
