Introduction

This article explains the Varicella Dx in immunization reports.

In most immunization reports, there are fields for dates when animmunization of Varicella was given. There are also fields for when theDx of Varicella was assessed.
There exist several Dxs that match "description like '%chicken%' or description like '%varic%' order by description"

When selecting Dxs for Varicella, do not select ones with the word "Vaccine"
