Growth Chart Rules

10458

Introduction

Symptom

This article explains the growth chart rules in Version 2.0 and Version 4.0. Unfortnately this is not configurable as it is hard coded and it is configured based on data and information provided to us from the CDC. 

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

1) If the dob is less than 3 years then it shows infant graph with data plots from 0-36 months
2) If dob is over 3 years then it shows children graph with data plot from 24 months to 20 years
3) If over 20 years then no graph ..it just shows the data
