How to find patient attachments in CPRFiles with cpr_id

10460

Introduction

This article explains how to find a patient attachment in the CPRFiles directory with the patient's cpr_id

Symptom


Cause


Workaround


Resolution

Under the CPRFiles folder there are folders numbered 0-9. With the lasttwo digits of the patient's cpr_id, open folders by those numbers. Forexample, if the patient's cpr_id is 35209, the patient's attachmentswill be located at d:\jmj\cprfiles\0\9\ and they will all have a namelike 35209*.
