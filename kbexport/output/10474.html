Print name and sig of treatment owner instead of encounter owner

10474

Introduction

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

On the report in question, inactivate near the bottom the commands
"Treatment Open Encounter" with a display script_id of "Encounter Owned By Signature"
and "Encounter Owned By"


In front of the first, Insert New Command and pick (in the treatment context)  "Owned by property"
    property = signature_stamp
    width = 2000
    height = 500

In front of the second, Insert New Command (in the treatment context)  "Owned By"
