How to tell which encounter workplans a practice uses

10498

Introduction

Sometimes a practice wants a change made to "all of their encounters".  Obviously, we can't edit every single encounter workplan, and it's not safe to go by the short lists.  Here is the SQL query that shows which encounter workplans have been used and how often.

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

Notes

SELECT TOP 20 c_Workplan.description,
Count (p_Patient_WP.patient_workplan_id) AS CountOfpatient_workplan_id
FROM c_Workplan INNER JOIN p_Patient_WP
ON c_Workplan.workplan_id = p_Patient_WP.workplan_id
GROUP BY p_Patient_WP.workplan_type, c_Workplan.description
HAVING p_Patient_WP.workplan_type='encounter'
ORDER BY Count(p_Patient_WP.patient_workplan_id) DESC;
