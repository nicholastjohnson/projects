Incomplete PMH Report

10501

Introduction

Some databases that went out to client sites had incomplete display scripts attached to the "PMH report".

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

Go to EncounterPro Configuration
Display Scripts
With "Patient" selected on the right, click "Display Script Name"
Type "PMH" in the box and click "OK" or hit "Enter"
Click on PMH Report
Click "Display/Edit"
Create a local version and set the local version to Active, if necessary
Hilight the Local Version and click "Edit Display Script"

Scroll down and click on the "Patient - treatments" command
Click "Disable Command"
Click "Insert New Command"
Click "Yes" if a dialogue box pops up
With "Patient" selected at the top of the screen, scroll down and click "observations", then OK
Click on the box next to "Observation"
Change the Treatment Type to PFSH and pick the PFSH that they use (example, Child PFSH)
Click OK, Next
Leave all other attributes blank
Click OK and Finished
