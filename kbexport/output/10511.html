Allowing units to be dosed in decimals or fractions

10511

Introduction

These things are set by unit - here's an example:
"Adderall XR comes in capsules, but the way the drug is manufactured, we can instruct people to use 1.5 capsules and they will get the correct dose. Where in the tables do I need to change capsules to allow us to order 1.5, because now it rounds up to 2."

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

Go to c_unit, find the unit_id, go to the pretty_fraction and choose UNIT, HALF, THIRD, QUARTER, TENTH, HUNDREDTH, THOUSANDTH, or MILLIONTH.  Then go to the display mask and pick HALF or QUARTER, which will round the entry to the nearest half or quarter and will display the fractional amount. Also, you can put in the desired combination of # signs and 0s, so that a number will display, if one exists, in the space where a # sign is, or if you put a 0 in the mask, either the number in that place will display, or a 0.

Examples:
Mask       Entered number        Displayed amount
#.#            3.41                        3.4
#.##          3.41                        3.41
#.##          0.41                        .41
0.##          0.41                        0.41
0.00          1.2                          1.20
HALF       1.5                          1 1/2
