Introduction

To keep Medicaid codes from billing, go to c_authority and look at the coding_component_id field.  Delete just that field (not the row) anywhere you see JMJ_MEDICAID.

Where Medicaid billing codes come from:

Find the patient's insurance code (or authority_id) in p_patient_authority.authority_id

Look for that authority_id in c_authority, then find the coding_component_id

Find that component_id in c_component_attribute

Inthe atttribute table, there should be a list_id_normal and alist_id_abnormal.  The lists for these are found inc_age_range_procedure, for their respective list_ids.  The age rangesin c_age_range_procedure are linked to c_age_range, and theprocedure_ids are found in c_procedure. 

Remember,the prodecure_ids in  c_age_range_procedure are NOT the cpt codes!!! They may be the same in some cases, but be SURE to double check inc_procedure!!!
