Resending Labs using CMA

10529

Introduction

Go to the Customer Mgmt Application located at https://www.jmjtech.com/mgmt/

 

Enter in your user name and password

 

From the Main Page you can either enter in the name of the customer or select “Customer Mgmt” link

 

 

 

When you click on “Customer Mgmt” link you get list of customers to select from. Select your customer by clicking the Practice ID by the practice’s name.

 

 

 

After you have selected the customer, you will go to the customer’s page. Scroll down to the “Messages” portion of the customer page.

 

 

 

This is a list of the Messages (lab result) sent by Epie to the customer. You can click on the check box for individual or multiple labs under the Resend column. Then click on the Resend button at the top of the column. If generally takes about 2 minutes for the customer to receive the message. If you refresh your browser, you will see the new message with a Status of processing or ready in red text. When the Status is “Complete” in the green text then the lab result has been received by the customer and is either in their “Scan/Post” utilities or mapped to the correspondent lab in the customer’s Epro.
