How Drug Interaction Databases are Updated

10533

Introduction

This article explains how EncounterPRO will provide the ability to update drug interaction databases.

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

EncounterPRO accesses, using a Web service provided by NewCrop LLC (http://www.newcroprx.com), First DataBank drug content, including drug interaction data.

NewCrop receives monthly update CDs from First Databank and immediately updates the content accessible by Web service.

Below was received from NewCrop on 5/15/06:

Official NewCrop language:

NewCrop receives updates from First Data Bank on a monthly basis.  After an internal Quality Control and Validation process (usually 3 weeks), NewCrop makes the updates available for download by our business partners.
