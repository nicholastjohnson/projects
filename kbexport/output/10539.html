Protection of Health Information over open Networks

10539

Introduction

This article describes how, when communication is necessary over open networks, EncounterPRO protects any PHI communicated against confidentiality failures and integrity failures.

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

Any commication of PHI over open networks is protected by 128-bit or higher encryption, through the use of either IPSec VPN or SSL.  This ensures that only the intended recipient(s) of PHI are able to view it.
