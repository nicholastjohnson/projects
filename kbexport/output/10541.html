Protection of User Passwords

10541

Introduction

This article describes how EncounterPRO handles passwords in such a way that passwords are never saved or communicated over a network in any form other than encrypted or hashed using SHA1.

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

Only hashes of passwords are saved for the EncounterPRO application.  Nowhere does the system store the password in a method that could allow for the reconstruction of the password by anyone.  EncounterPRO relies on the SHA1 hashing algorithm.
