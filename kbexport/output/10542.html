Introduction

This article describes how EncounterPRO handles passwords in such a way that passwords are never communicated over a network in any form other than encrypted or hashed using SHA1.

 

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

The EncounterPRO application transmits passwords over the internet only with the protection of SSL 128-bit encryption.  Only SHA1 hashed passwords are transmitted over other public or private networks.
