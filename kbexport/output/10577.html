Mapping auto-selecting computers to exam rooms

10577

Introduction

You can set a certain computer (or a certain thin client login) to display just the view of its preferred room when the user first logs in, and not the entire office view.

(Of course, the user can click on any tab on the right side of the screen and then look at any room or patient - this only sets the initial default view.)

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

Go to Configuration and Preferences. 
Set the level at the bottom to Computer. 
At the bottom of the screen next to  -Preference Key-  pick the computer that you want to configure. 

Click on Computer configuration on the left, and page down to  -"Preferred Room- 
Click on that and select which room you want the computer to be "mapped" to. 

Click on  -General Preferences-  on the left and page down to  -Special Auto Room Select (do not use)-. 
Click on that, click in the white box and type  T  and click Finished.
