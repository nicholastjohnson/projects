Finding the most frequently used observations in a treatment type

10580

Introduction

SELECT TOP 10 c_observation_description,
Count(p_treatment_item.treatment_id) AS CountOfTreat
FROM c_observation
INNER JOIN p_treatment_item ON
c_observation.observation_id = p_treatment_item.observation_id
INNER JOIN c_observation_treatment_type
ON c_observation.observation_id = c_observation_treatment_type.observation_id
WHERE c_observation_treatment_type.treatment_type like '%PFSH%'
GROUP BY c_observation.description, c_observation_treatment_type.treatment_type
order by count (p_treatment_item.treatment_id) DESC
