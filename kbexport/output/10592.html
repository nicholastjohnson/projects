Printing ICD-9 of a treatment's assessment

Introduction

SELECT
c_assessment_definition.description,
c_Assessment_Definition.icd_9_code
FROM p_Treatment_Item LEFT JOIN p_Assessment_Treatment
ON p_Treatment_Item.treatment_id = p_Assessment_Treatment.treatment_id
LEFT JOIN c_Assessment_Definition RIGHT JOIN p_Assessment
ON c_Assessment_Definition.assessment_id = p_Assessment.assessment_id
ON p_Assessment_Treatment.problem_id = p_Assessment.problem_id
AND p_Assessment_Treatment.cpr_id = p_Assessment.cpr_id
WHERE p_Treatment_Item.treatment_id= %treatment_id%
