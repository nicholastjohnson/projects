I added an observation tag (OBFollowUp) to OB Physicals that are children of non-OB physicals since the results of OB Physicals are reported separately in the OB AP Report.   For example, in the Gynecologic Physical, the observation tag 'OBFollowUp' was added to the child observation called "OB Follow-Up Exam (without cervix)."

I configured the OB AP Report to display abnormal results for Physical Exams excluding observations where the observation tag is 'OBFollowUp' . The Patient Treatment display is further limited by a Treatment RTF query as follows:

SELECT 1 FROM p_treatment_item ti WITH (NOLOCK)

INNER JOIN p_observation o WITH (NOLOCK)

ON ti.observation_id = o.observation_id AND ti.treatment_id = o.treatment_id

INNER JOIN p_observation_result obr WITH (NOLOCK)

ON o.observation_sequence = obr.root_observation_sequence

WHERE ti.cpr_id = '%cpr_id%'

AND ti.treatment_id = %treatment_id%

AND ti.treatment_type = 'PHYSICAL'

AND ti.observation_id NOT IN

(SELECT domain_item FROM c_domain WHERE domain_id = 'OBPHYSICAL')

AND ((ti.treatment_status IS NULL) OR (ti.treatment_status = 'CLOSED'))

AND o.parent_observation_sequence IS NULL

AND obr.abnormal_flag = 'Y' AND obr.current_flag = 'Y'
