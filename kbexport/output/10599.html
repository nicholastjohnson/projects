Closing Out of Office ToDo items for a user or role

10599

Introduction

In this situation, there were massive amounts of ToDos for a LabCorp Coordinator role, and they wanted all of the ToDos closed for labs older than 10/1/2006.

To use this query, open the access db, click on Query, double click on Create Query in Design view, click Close, then click on SQL in the upper left corner, then paste the below query in the box:

UPDATE dbo_p_Patient_WP_Item SET dbo_p_Patient_WP_Item.status = "COMPLETED", dbo_p_Patient_WP_Item.completed_by = "SUPPORT", dbo_p_Patient_WP_Item.end_date = #10/17/2006#
WHERE (((dbo_p_Patient_WP_Item.status)="Dispatched") AND ((dbo_p_Patient_WP_Item.owned_by)="!LabCoCoord") AND ((dbo_p_Patient_WP_Item.created)<#10/1/2006#));

Then click on View in the top menu and pick Design view.

Change the three circled fields to meet your needs so that

End Date = today

Owned_by = user_id or role_id (found in c_user and c_role)

Created = the latest date for which they want the ToDos closed
