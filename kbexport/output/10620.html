Remove Sick Well Waiting Room

10620

Introduction

See also Article #10208

If the practice only has one waiting room and you want to remove the menu that appears when you manually check-in a patient that asks which waiting room (Sick or Well) you want to put the patient into:

 

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

Go to the database (cpr or cpr_40)

1. Open the o_rooms table.

2. Change one of the waiting rooms from status of OK to NA.

3. Change the Description of the other waiting room from Sick/Well Waiting Room to simply Waiting Room. Close the table.

The change should be in effect immediately. You should not have to re-start Epro.
