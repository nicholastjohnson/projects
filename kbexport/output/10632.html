Stuck Service Bar in version 2

10632

Introduction

Symptom

User clicks on a service bar (almost always a testperform or administer immunization) and nothing happens (except they take over the service bar.)

Cause

There is a mismatch between the service bar and the treatment. (I don't know why this happens)

Workaround

none

Resolution

Find the patient's cpr_id in p_patient.Filter for that cpr_id in p_treatment_item and find the treatment_id attached to the "stuck" service bar.Filter for the cpr_id (NOT the treatment_id) in p_encounter_log and filter-exclude rows with the status of COMPLETE. There should be a row with a status of READY with a description matching the treatment. If you look at the treatment_id for that row, it will probably be one or a few numbers off of the treatment_id you found in p_treatment_item. Replace the treatment_id value in p_Encounter_Log with the value from p_treatment_item.
