How the Florida Registry handles the immunization data received from EncounterPRO

Introduction

this article explains how the Florida Registry handles the immunization data received from EncounterPRO Customer.

Workaround

Resolution

Notes

EncounterPRO periodically (default is 10 minutes interval) transfers the vaccine data from the EncounterPRO Customer to its Central Integration Engine (EPIE) where it gets transformed to the format different state registries understands and then submitted to them via states preferred transmission protocol (SFTP / HTTPS etc).

Florida Registry takes 24 hours or next day once the file is received that the client is able to view it in Florida SHOTS website. The registry can not process the files that has no address information and so all the JMJ customers are advised to fill the Patient Address and other basic informations that is necessary in order for the vaccine messages to successfully post into the FL registry system.

Feel free to contact Diane (Diane_McLeod@doh.state.fl.us) at the FL registry directly if you have any specific questions.
