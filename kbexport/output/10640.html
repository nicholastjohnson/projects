Default the name of a lab company to appear automatically in the Collect or Refer Menu

10640

Introduction

 

This article shows the steps that will automatically set a lab company’s name to appear in Specimen Sent to: or depending on the practice, Patient Referred To:.  This way, the clinical staff does not need to click on the property bar of the collect screen and can simply click collected and finish to order lab.

 

Hint: Before beginning, it would be very helpful to log into practice site, go to Microsoft Access and open cpr.mdb to view dbo_c_consultant (shown below) in tables and make a note of description field and consultant_id field of the lab company you are defaulting.  This will avoid having to exit out of everything while in the middle of configuring to look up the consultant_id and consultant description.

Resolution

Perform these steps: 

  1. Click “Configuration”
  2. Click “Workplans”
  3. Use the following search criteria to search for “Lab, Ongoing Clinical Staff Collect or Send/Refer Out (Integration)”: “Workplan Type” set to “Treatment” and change the “Specialty” button to “All”.  When found click on “Lab, Ongoing Clinical Staff Collect or Send/Refer Out (Integration), then click on “Display Edit” button. (If this workplan does not appear, the practice may be on the wrong config. level. Notify Support.)
  4. Click on “Edit Work Plan” with the local version highlighted.  (If there is no local version created, click “Create Local Copy” button. Note: If this doesn’t work run Hot Fix 94.)  Hint: Three steps will already be included in this workplan:  Collect Specimen, Wait, and Review Lab.  You will be adding two more steps to workplan which will default the lab comapny's name. 
  5. Click “Add Step” at bottom.  A pop box will appear, then enter the description “Set Perform Mode” and click “OK”.
  6. Click on “Set Perform Mode”, which is now step #4, and then click the “Move Up” button (black arrow) to move up a step.  Do this until “Set Perform Mode” is the first step, should be three times.
  7. Click “Set Perform Mode” again and then click “Delay Step” button and click ok.  You will see in box next to “Set Perform Mode”.
  8. Click “Set Perform Mode” another time then “Edit Step”
  9. Click Add Service and select “New Note”.
  10. A pop box appears.  Insert the following to the corresponding field:

a)      Context Object: select “Treatment” (click next)

b)      Standard Note Type: LEAVE THIS FIELD BLANK (click next)

c)      Special Note Type: type in “Property” in white dialog box (click next)

d)      Preconfigured Treatment Titles or Keys: select “Specimen Sent To: (click next)

e)      Other Note Title or Key: type in “Specimen Sent To:” in white dialog box (click next)

f)        Note Text: LabCorp (example only) (NOTE: enter lab company’s name the way it is listed in tables c consultant “consultant description” not the consultant id.  (click finish)

  11.   You should be at the Workplan Step Definition screen then click “New Note” then “Ordered For” and

          select Clinical Staff.

  1. Click “New Note” again then “Edit Flags”.  “Counts Toward Step Completion” and “Perform Immediately” should be checked YES all other flags NO or NA.  Flag titled “Where:” needs to read In Office.  Click “OK” when completed.
  2. Click “OK” again to return to the Workplan Definition screen.
  3. Click “Add Step” at bottom.  A pop box will appear, then enter the description “Set Consultant ID” and click “OK”.
  4. Click on “Set Consultant ID”, which is now step #5, and then click the “Move Up” button (black arrow) to move up a step.  Do this until “Set Consultant ID” is the second step, should be three times.
  5. Click “Set Consultant ID” again and then click “Delay Step” button and click ok.  You will see in box next to “Set Consultant ID”
  6.  Click “Set Consultant ID” another time then click “Edit Step”.
  7.  Click Add Service and select “New Note”.
  8.  A pop box appears.  Insert the following to the corresponding field:

            a)      Context Object: select “Treatment” (click next)

b)      Standard Note Type: LEAVE THIS FIELD BLANK (click next)

c)      Special Note Type: type in “Property” in white dialog box (click next)

d)      Preconfigured Treatment Titles or Keys: LEAVE THIS FIELD BLANK (click next)

e)      Other Note Title or Key: type in “Consultant_ID” in white dialog box (click next)

f)        Note Text: LAB_CORP (example only) (NOTE: enter lab company’s name the way it is listed in table’s c consultant “consultant_id” not the consultant description. (click finish)

  1.   Repeat steps 11 and 12.  All is completed.
