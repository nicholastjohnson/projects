Can't take over an encounter in version 2

10649

Introduction

Error message "You may not take over an encounter which is already owned by (x)"

Symptom

When a doctor or NP tries to take over another encounter, they get this error message and it does not let them take over the encounter or the examination service.

Cause

The user does not have a provider_id in c_user

Workaround

Resolution

Find the user in c_provider, copy the provider_id and paste it in the provider_id field in c_user.
