Print blank pages after Prescriptions

10662

Introduction

This article is to be referenced when blank pages are trailing the rx's during printing.

Symptom

two pages after rx is printed.

Cause

The double signature line is enabled in the tables instead of single signature line.

Workaround

Change the prescription size from 2 signature line to 1 signature line.

Look for dw_presctiption_size_20 in both c_report_attribute, and o_report_attribute. make sure theres no "2" next to "prescription".


                                             This is what it looks like originally
report_idattribute_sequenceattributevalue
{ED21F61C-D7A2-40EF-9022-2FE20658839D}23dataobjectdw_prescription2_size_20

This is what it should look like afterwards
report_idattribute_sequenceattributevalue
{ED21F61C-D7A2-40EF-9022-2FE20658839D}23dataobjectdw_prescription_size_20
