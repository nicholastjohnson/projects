Prescription heading too high

10663

Introduction

This article explains how to lower the heading on the prescription in the event that its printing off the paper.

Symptom

Office address or Office name printing off paper at top.

Cause

The top print margin for the paper is not set or is set too low.

Workaround

Increase the blank print area above the header my adding these rows and adjusting the value to fit. add these rows to o_report_attribute
