Automatically send patient to billing review holding room

10672

Introduction

The practice would like the patient to go straight to abilling review holding room after the provider finishes the examination.

Symptom

Cause

Workaround

Resolution

Start by creating a new room called Billing Review under theHolding tab (see KB article # 10208 on creating rooms.  The room typeshould be $EXAMINATION.  If the practice has more than one office, andthey would like this step for all offices, create the room for eachoffice).  Find out if the practice would like Epro to behave this way forall encounter types, or just sick/well, etc.  If you want to change theworkplan for sick visits, go to Configuration àEncounter Types, select EST Patient Office Visit, and select Edit to see whichworkplan it is using. 

Now go to Configuration àWorkplans, change the workplan type to Encounter at the top of the screen, findthe workplan you just confirmed is attached to the EST Patient OfficeVisit.  Select the workplan, click “Copy” from the menu that appears andname the copy something specific (Bill Rev Standard EncC Office Visit EST PT) andmemorable.  Edit the new workplan that you just created.  If the laststep is supervisory review, delete that step.  Don’t click Add Step, butrather “Final Step”.  Click on the step, select edit, add a new servicecalled Billing Review by Supervisor. 

Click the service, select OrderedFor.  Ask the practice who will be completing the review.  If the service will be going to one role, such as Admin Billing Review, select "Any Admin Billing" from the list of roles.  If the review will be going to the supervisor of a NP, CA,or other certified staff member, change the button at the top from Users toSpecial and select "Encounter Supervisor" as the service owner. Click OK.  Only if you have selected Encounter Supervisor (you do not haveto do this for roles such as Admin Billing), click on your service again andselect Edit Criteria.  Click on the button for Workplan Owner. Change the button at the top from Users to Roles.  Select Any NP, PA orCert Staff.  Click OK.  *Be sure that the NP’s/PA’s have supervisorsassigned in their user profile.  Click onthe service again and select Edit Flags.  Change Perform Immediately toYes.  Click OK.

Click OK again so that you are back on the screen where you are viewingall the steps for the workplan.  Click on your new step and select RoomAssignment.  Select Examination on the Left, then click on the buttonlabeled "Default Room Type".  If the practice has more than one office, select the first office and a list ofrooms will appear; among them will be the Billing Review room you createdearlier.  Select that room.  If the practice would like this step tohappen in both offices, click the top button where you previously selected thefirst office.  Select the second office and highlight your Billing Reviewroom again.  Click ok.  You have now completed your workplan. To test your workplan, create a test encounter type, assign your new workplanto the test encounter, and check a test patient in.

Now find the encounter type (EST Patient Office Visit in this case) that has the original workplan(the one you copied) assigned to it.  Remove the original workplan andassign your new workplan.

To ensure that all the previous step are correct, you cannotassign your new workplan to all encounter types.  You will have to seewhich workplan each encounter type is using, make a copy of that workplan, addthe step, then assign your new workplan to the encounter type.  You can dothis by going to Configuration à Encounter Types, find the encounter, click Edit. Select the current workplan and delete it.  Then select the Add Workplanbutton at the bottom and attach your newly created workplan.
