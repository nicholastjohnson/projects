Treatment Attachment Caption printing

10682

Introduction

There's a new attribute to the Treatment Attachment command (and I suppose to all of the other Attachment contexts) that prints out information about the attachment along with it. Examples are %attachment attached_by(user.user_full_name)%%attachment attachment_date%%attachment attachment_tag%or any other p.attachment field name, I think. Captions can be separated by carriage returns or spaces.
