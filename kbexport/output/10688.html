Scanning into Word 2007 New KB Article

10688

Introduction

Symptom

The insert> from scanner button is missing in 2007.

Cause

Create the following Macro (View > Macro > View Macros > Macros in Normal.dotm > Type 'scan' as the name > Create > Paste in code:

Workaround

Sub InsertFromScanner()On Error Resume NextWordBasic.InsertImagerScanEnd Sub

Resolution

You can then 'Customise the Quick Access Toolbar' > choose commands from: 'Macros' and ADD. Then Modify it to give it a distinctive icon.This will give you the 'insert from scanner' box with one click from the Quick Access Toolbar (QAT).
