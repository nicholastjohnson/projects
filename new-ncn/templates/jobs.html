Marketing Specialist

How do you know whether you love coding if you have no way to learn? How can you even start solving the problems in your world if you don’t even own a computer?

At Codestarter we’re working to create the next generation of coders and empower them to begin building the future. We believe this starts by getting a laptop in the hands of every child who wants to learn how to code, but who can’t afford a computer. Codestarter crowdfunds laptops, and we partner with organizations that teach coding in order to reach diverse populations of children worldwide.

We’re hiring for a part-time or full-time marketing specialist who can help us tell the world about our cause.

Who are you?

Work experience:

Maybe you’re in tech, and you’re looking to measure your success based on people helped. Or maybe you’ve worked on a nonprofit development team, and you’re ready to be the one in charge. From the moment you begin at Codestarter, your work will immediately impact kids in need. This is an opportunity for you to craft our entire strategy for reaching out to new donors.

Ideally, you’ve worked in tech, at a startup, preferably a B2C, in Silicon Valley or SF. Previously you’ve helped develop a marketing strategy and worn many hats.

Skills:

  • You’re a social media pro—Twitter, FB, Instagram, Blogs—you use them yourself, and you’ve maintained active accounts for your previous company. You know how to analyze how Codestarter’s doing and can create campaigns to increase engagement.
  • Google docs, Dropbox, Mailchimp… you know the basics. Bonus if you use GitHub.
  • You know your way around Google Analytics or another system.
  • You’re a talented writer and a detail-oriented researcher.
  • Bonus if you’ve got some design skills.
  • You’ve never accidentally misspelled anything in a tweet. Ok, you did maybe once, but that’s it.
  • You use an Oxford comma and don’t double space between sentences. Seriously.

Work style:

  • You’re not afraid to take responsibility for big projects and follow through, while discussing ideas with the rest of the team.
  • You dive in and figure things out on your own, but you don’t go radio-silent for a week to shave a yak.
  • You argue based on data and experience, but you’re willing to consider crazy ideas, implement quickly, and change direction if need be.
  • When choosing between high quality work and fast production, you somehow manage to do both.
  • You don’t lose focus or motivation working independently when team members are away.
  • You can crank out work to meet deadlines that pop up.
  • You enjoy working asynchronously over chat.
  • You aren’t afraid to talk to strangers in coffee houses for user-testing, and you’d love to chat up people at meetups and conferences and tell them about our cause, “Laptops for kid coders.”
  • Other people consider you outgoing, but they’d also say that you’re sensitive to issues of diversity, gender, age, sexuality, ability, class, and belief.

How to apply

Please send a résumé and cover letter to info@codestarter.org, subject: Marketing specialist. We’re accepting applications until September 1.
