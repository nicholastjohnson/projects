About

Nick was introduced to computers the same way most people were in the 90s. A shiny disk arrived in his mailbox from AOL and connected him to the entire world. Fast forward 20 years, and Nick has done a little bit of everything in the world of IT. Currently, Nick works on two healthcare IT startups that he helped create, teaches development to kids, acts as vCIO for several small businesses, and is the on-call geek for many people -his family and friends included.

Nick was born and raised in East Cobb - attending Blackwell Elementary, McCleskey Middle, and Sprayberry High. After starting college and his family, Nick and his wife moved back to East Cobb from Atlanta. They have 3 little boys, a dog, a cat, and countless lizards and bugs that his boys want to keep. Nick's two older sons are already learning development skills that will last a lifetime. When they aren't working on technology, Nick and his family enjoy exploring the many parks and trails in the region and biking together.

Backup and Disaster Recovery

If you've ever lost a drive or laptop, you know how painful it can be, especially if you have irreplacable pictures of your friends and family on them. Technology has advanced so quickly, that you can store every digital file you own online, securely for only a few dollars a month.

There are hundreds of software services out there that promise to keep all of your personal information backed up, but they are costly and complicated. Let Nick take the wheel and get you to a state of calm in knowing that your data is safe and secure regardless of what happens to your computer.

If you've already lost some data, STOP! Don't do another thing on that computer. If you're on that computer now, turn it off, NOW. No, seriously! Pull the plug! Take out the battery! We'll be here when you get back from a different computer.

Good, you made it back. Okay, there is a chance that your data is recoverable. It isn't easy, and it may be time consuming. But, there's a chance. And, when your son's first birthday pictures are hanging in the balance, you have to try.

Nick has successfully recovered lost pictures and videos from USB sticks, hard drives and computers that have crashed. It doesn't always work, but it could.

Let Nick get you on a backup plan, so you'll never have to worry about losing precious data again.
